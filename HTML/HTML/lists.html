Step-by-Step Maggie Recipe using Ordered List

  1. Boil water Pour 1.5 to 2 cups of water into a pan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add Maggi noodles Open the Maggi packet and add the noodle cake to the boiling water
  3. Add tastemaker Add the Masala Tastemaker (the spice packet inside the Maggi pack) to the water.
  4. Stir well Mix the noodles and masala evenly with a spoon or fork.
  5. Cook for 1–2 minutes Let it cook uncovered, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  6. Add vegetables (optional) Add chopped vegetables like onions, tomatoes, capsicum, or peas for a healthier version.
  7. Cook for 2 more minutes Let the vegetables and noodles cook together until soft. You can add a bit more water if needed.
  8. Check consistency Decide if you want it dry or slightly soupy — cook accordingly.
  9. Turn off the heat Once the noodles are cooked and the water is mostly absorbed, switch off the stove
  10. Serve hot Transfer to a bowl, garnish with coriander (optional), and enjoy your hot Maggi!
